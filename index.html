Industry Problems

Trade finance faces systemic inefficiencies, high costs, and security vulnerabilities, leading to a $1.7 trillion financing gap:

  • 📄 Excessive manual paperwork slows down processing times, taking weeks instead of hours.
  • 🔍 Fraud risks: counterfeit invoices, identity fraud, and double financing scams.
  • 💰 High intermediary fees increase costs, limiting SME participation.
  • 🌎 Global regulatory fragmentation complicates cross-border transactions.
  • 🖥️ Lack of digitalization leads to inefficient reconciliation and processing delays.

What is FinTrust?
