What is FinTrust?

FinTrust is a blockchain-powered trade finance ecosystem designed to enhance transparency, reduce fraud, and unlock liquidity for global trade.

Features

  • 🔗 Secure blockchain ledger for immutable transactions.
  • ⚡ Real-time transaction processing and settlement.
  • 🛡️ AI-driven fraud detection and compliance automation.
  • 🌎 Seamless integration with global trade networks.

Implementation

  • 🚀 Phase 1: Establish partnerships with financial institutions.
  • 🔍 Phase 2: Develop AI-powered compliance automation.
  • 💰 Phase 3: Expand access to SME financing globally.

Industry Problems

  • 📄 Excessive manual paperwork slows down processing times.
  • 🔍 Fraud risks: counterfeit invoices, identity fraud, and double financing scams.
  • 💰 High intermediary fees increase costs, limiting SME participation.
  • 🌎 Global regulatory fragmentation complicates cross-border transactions.
  • 🖥️ Lack of digitalization leads to inefficient reconciliation and delays.

Market Opportunity

  • 📊 The global trade finance market is valued at $8 trillion annually.
  • 📉 $1.7 trillion in unmet financing needs restricts trade expansion.
  • 🏦 90% of banks are exploring blockchain solutions to modernize operations.
  • 🌍 Emerging markets need inclusive financing solutions to tap into global trade.

Technical Feasibility

  • 🔄 Built on Hyperledger Fabric and R3 Corda for high-security transactions.
  • ⚡ Smart contracts reduce transaction time from 10 days to 24 hours.
  • 🧠 AI-driven risk assessment enhances SME financing approvals.
  • 🛡️ Decentralized ledger eliminates fraud risks and boosts transparency.
  • 🔍 AML & KYC integration ensures compliance with global regulations.
