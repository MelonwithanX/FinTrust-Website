Because Trade Finance Should Just Work.

Industry Problems

Global trade finance faces inefficiencies, fraud, and high costs, creating a $1.7 trillion financing gap. Challenges include:

  • Slow processing times (5-10 days for Letters of Credit)
  • Manual documentation and verification causing bottlenecks
  • Fraud risks: duplicate invoices, falsified documents, and identity fraud
  • Fragmented AML and KYC compliance requirements
  • Exclusion of SMEs due to lack of credit access

Market Opportunity

  • Global trade finance market: $8 trillion annually
  • $1.7 trillion in unmet financing needs
  • 90% of banks are exploring blockchain solutions

Technical Feasibility

  • Built on Hyperledger Fabric and R3 Corda
  • Smart contracts reduce transaction time from 10 days to 24 hours
  • AI-powered risk assessment enhances SME financing
  • Decentralized trade ledger prevents fraud and improves transparency

Revenue Model

  • Transaction Fees - 0.5% per trade
  • Subscription-based fraud detection tools for banks
  • Revenue from tokenized invoice trading
  • Cross-border payment fees using stablecoins
