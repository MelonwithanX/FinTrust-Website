Why Trade Finance Needs Innovation

Global trade finance is plagued by inefficiencies, high costs, and fraud. $1.7 trillion in financing requests go unmet annually due to outdated systems. FinTrust is here to change that.

  • LC processing takes 5-10 days
  • Manual documentation & checks cause delays
  • Fraud risks: duplicate invoices, identity theft
  • Regulatory compliance is fragmented
  • High costs exclude SMEs from financing

Our Solution

Powered by blockchain, FinTrust offers:

  • Smart Contract Payments - Automates LC transactions, reducing time from 10 days to 24 hours.
  • Decentralized Ledger - Immutable records prevent fraud and provide real-time transparency.
  • AI-Powered Risk Scoring - Uses blockchain-based analytics to help SMEs access financing.
  • Tokenized Trade Assets - Converts invoices and LCs into digital assets for instant liquidity.
  • Cross-Border Stablecoin Payments - Reduces reliance on SWIFT, minimizing forex risks.
